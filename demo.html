How It Works

Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup. Choose between the Timer tab for productivity tracking or the Transform tab to apply themes to any webpage.

Medieval Theme

Transport yourself to a time of knights and castles. The Medieval theme applies parchment-style backgrounds, serif fonts, and a warm brown color scheme to any webpage you visit.

Kawaii Theme

Embrace the cuteness! The Kawaii theme transforms pages with soft pink backgrounds, rounded corners, and playful anime-inspired styling that makes browsing more fun.

Installation

Download the extension from the Chrome Web Store or load it manually in developer mode. Once installed, click the extension icon to start transforming your web experience.
